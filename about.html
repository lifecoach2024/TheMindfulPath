My Real Life Experience

SUCCESS STORy

VAIDEHI SHUKLA

Life Coach

I am an ambitious workaholic, but apart from that, pretty simple person.

Three years ago, I was a homemaker with no job, limited English proficiency, and a fear of public speaking. Fast forward to today, and I am a certified life coach, living proof of undeniable transformation. But the journey to this point wasn’t easy.

It all began with my family’s immigration process to Canada. We received our visas, but just as we were about to book our tickets, the pandemic hit. Travel from India to Canada was banned, and everything seemed hopeless. It felt as though our dreams had come to a complete halt. During this time, I was actively learning life coaching techniques. As the situation seemed more difficult, I decided to apply the techniques I was learning—manifestation and healing—to our situation.

The first major challenge was securing priority COVID-19 vaccinations, which were limited. Once we successfully got vaccinated, the travel ban from India to Canada still stood. However, we discovered an alternative route through Mexico. Everything was set, but the path wasn’t without its risks. We had to stop in Mexico for two days and take a COVID test there. Until we passed, we couldn’t continue our journey to Canada.

My husband was worried, but I applied the techniques I had learned and reassured him, saying, “Nothing will happen; we will make it.” We bravely took the route, passing the COVID test in India and again in Mexico. Within three months, everything shifted. After passing all the required tests, we were able to travel and finally arrived in Canada with our little daughter.

At the same time, I was also working on returning to the workforce after a 10-year gap. I started by taking a labor job at a coffee shop. It was tough, but I wasn’t satisfied with just that. I applied the techniques Manifestation & Healing , I eventually secured a job in a multinational company (MNC). I started with a salary of $3,500 per month. After receiving my first 3.5% salary increase, I still felt unsatisfied. I decided to apply the same Manifestation and Healing techniques I had been learning in life coaching to manifest further success. The result? I received another raise, bringing my salary to $4,000 per month. While these increments might not seem large in some industries, the true achievement was that I had earned back-to-back raises in the competitive Canadian market after a 10-year professional gap. This experience solidified my belief in the power of manifestation and mindset techniques.

But the challenges didn’t end there. When I started my job at the MNC, it started with walking to the bus station, followed by taking buses, metros, trains, and another bus just to reach work. The same exhausting route awaited me on the way back. It was tough, demanding, and required so much effort that it became incredibly hard to manage. I began manifesting the desire for my own car, but there was one major issue: I didn’t know how to drive.

I had no knowledge of driving at all. In fact, my driving instructor doubted my ability and even stopped the lessons midway, telling me I didn’t even know the basics of operating a car. I was starting from zero. But I didn’t give up. I applied the same techniques I had been learning in life coaching—manifestation, perseverance, and self-belief. Within just one month, I passed my driving exam and obtained my full license. This achievement was monumental—not only because it was a major milestone for me, but also because it showed that persistence, manifestation, and the right mindset could turn what seemed like an impossible task into a reality.

As my own life transformed, I realized the power of the techniques I had learned and how valuable they could be in helping others. Inspired by my journey, I decided to share these tools with those seeking transformation. This led to the creation of The Mindful Path, where I now teach others how to break through their limitations and create their own transformations. From a homemaker with no job to a certified life coach, this journey has shown me that with the right mindset and tools, anything is possible.
