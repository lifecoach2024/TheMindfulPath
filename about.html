My Real Life Experience

SUCCESS STORy

VAIDEHI SHUKLA

Life Coach

I am an ambitious workaholic, but apart from that, pretty simple person.

"Three years ago, I was a homemaker with no job, limited English proficiency, and a significant fear of public speaking. Today, after embracing life coaching techniques, I have transformed into a confident speaker, fluent in both English and French. I secured a role in a multinational company (MNC) with a monthly salary of $3,000, despite having only 1.2 years of directly relevant experience after a 10-year gap. Within just three months, I achieved two back-to-back increments, pushing my salary above $4,000. While $4,000 may not seem significant in many fields, for me, it’s a huge accomplishment. Starting from zero, with a decade-long gap and the challenges of being a homemaker, this achievement represents how far I’ve come."

One notable achievement was my journey to obtain a driving license in Canada. Initially, I had no knowledge of driving, and my instructor even doubted my ability to drive, stopping the lessons midway. However, within just one month, I passed the driving exam and obtained a full license—an accomplishment that seemed impossible to many, but a testament to the effectiveness of my perseverance and coaching techniques.

Throughout my journey, I have not only experienced the power of manifestation but also the transformative effects of healing. One powerful instance involved my husband, who had been struggling with shoulder pain for a while. Without telling him, I began a healing process for him. After a few days, he came to me and asked, 'Did you do some magic? My pain is gone!' It was a moment of profound realization for me. In healing, it’s important not to inform the person you are healing, as it’s a subtle process. Yet, when someone notices the change themselves, you truly witness the power of healing

Many people are skeptical about healing and manifestation, especially in today’s world, but I firmly believe in their effectiveness. I’ve experienced it firsthand and seen how life-changing it can be.

Based on both my healing and manifestation experiences, I decided to start offering these services through my club. I want others to take advantage of these powerful tools and experience transformation for themselves."
